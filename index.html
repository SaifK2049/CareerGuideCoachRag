Before continuing

Private beta and AI processing

Masari sends relevant excerpts from the CV, job descriptions, and evidence you submit to an AI provider to create cited career analysis. The beta is experimental and does not provide employment advice.

Permanent action

Delete your Masari account

This permanently removes your account, CV files, jobs, evidence, analyses, and feedback. Export your data first if you need a copy.

Account security

Choose a new password

Use at least ten characters with upper-case, lower-case, and number characters. Do not reuse a password.
