Masari Private Beta Privacy Notice

This notice describes how the invitation-only Masari private beta handles the information you choose to submit. The beta operator is the person or organisation identified in your invitation. This notice must be reviewed with the operator’s legal identity and contact details before a wider public launch.

Information Masari processes

Masari stores your account email, career profile, CV text and uploaded PDF, target job paths, job descriptions, knowledge evidence, saved AI analyses, feedback, and limited operational audit events. If you join the waitlist, Masari stores the name and email address you submit so it can contact you about early access. It does not intentionally collect payment data during the free private beta.

Why it is processed

The information is used to provide your private workspace, retrieve relevant evidence, create cited career-gap analyses, secure the service, investigate failures, export your data, and respond to beta feedback. Masari does not sell your career data or use it for advertising.

AI processing and service providers

When you request an analysis, relevant excerpts from the CV, job descriptions, and evidence you submitted are sent server-side to an AI provider to generate embeddings and a cited response. Supabase provides authentication, database, private file storage, and server functions. Cloudflare provides web delivery and security. These providers process data only as needed to operate the beta under their applicable service terms.

Storage, access, and retention

Your workspace is separated by account-level database and storage rules. Other beta users cannot access it through the application. CVs, career data, and successful analyses are retained until you clear the workspace or delete the account. Temporary processing records may be removed earlier. Limited operational events may be retained for up to 90 days.

Your controls

You can export a JSON copy of your account data, clear workspace content, or permanently delete the account from Profile & CV. The JSON export includes extracted CV text but does not embed the original PDF binary. Reply to your beta invitation for access, correction, deletion, privacy, or support requests.

Security and limitations

Masari uses authenticated access, row-level database controls, private file storage, rate limits, and server-side provider keys. No system can guarantee absolute security. Avoid submitting unnecessary special-category personal data, identity documents, references’ private contact details, or confidential employer information.
